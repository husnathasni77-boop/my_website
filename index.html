Welcome to Abooka’s Chayakkada – where every cup of chai tells a story and every bite feels like home. Rooted in tradition and bursting with authentic flavors, our teashop is known for its signature banana fry paired perfectly with rich, flavorful beef curry – a combination that keeps our customers coming back for more. At Abooka’s, we celebrate the essence of local taste with a variety of freshly prepared fried snacks, served hot and crisp alongside our perfectly brewed tea. Whether you're stopping by for a quick break or a relaxed catch-up, our cozy space and soulful flavors promise a truly satisfying experience. Come, sip, snack, and savor the tradition at Abooka’s Chayakkada.
